The Young Scholar

Site Name

The Young Scholar (TYS) is the chosen site name for this project. It reflects the organization's mission to nurture and guide aspiring Pisay scholars through high-quality yet affordable tutorial services. The name is direct, professional, and clearly communicates the purpose of the website.

Site Purpose

The purpose of The Young Scholar website is to provide information about affordable and high-quality Pisay (Philippine Science High School) entrance exam preparation, to showcase the tutors, and to offer a way for parents and students to sign up for tutorials or review sessions.

Scenarios

  • What review programs are available to help my child prepare for the Pisay NCE?
  • Who are the tutors, and what qualifications do they have?
  • How do I register my child for a review session?

Color Schema

The color palette is based on the official TYS logo:

  • #1b2752 (Deep Navy) - Used for headers, navigation bar, and footer background.
  • #ff914d (Bright Orange) - Accent color for headings, buttons, and highlights.
  • #000000 (Black) - Used for body text to ensure high contrast and readability.
  • #acbfc4 (Muted Gray) - Used for secondary text and borders.

Typography

The site uses two Google Fonts:

  • Poppins - Used for headings (h1, h2, h3) for a modern, bold look.
  • Roboto - Used for body text and paragraphs for readability and simplicity.
